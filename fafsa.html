FAFSA Help

Step-by-step guidance to complete your FAFSA with confidence.

Why FAFSA?

The Free Application for Federal Student Aid (FAFSA) is the key to unlocking grants, loans, and work-study opportunities for college. Completing it early ensures you don’t miss out on financial aid.

How to Get Started

  1. Create an FSA ID at studentaid.gov.
  2. Gather documents: Social Security number, tax returns, bank statements.
  3. Log in to FAFSA.gov and start your application.
  4. List the colleges you’re applying to.
  5. Submit and check your Student Aid Report (SAR).

Tips for Success

  • Submit early — some aid is first-come, first-served.
  • Double-check your info to avoid delays.
  • Use the IRS Data Retrieval Tool for accuracy.
